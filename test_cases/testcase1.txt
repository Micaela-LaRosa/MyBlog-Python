Test Case 1: Creating a Blog Entry

Objective:
Verify that a user can create a new blog entry successfully.

Steps:

1. Log in as an authorized user.

2. Navigate to the blog creation page.

3. Fill out the form with title, subtitle, body text, and optionally an image.

4. Submit the form.

5. Verify that the new blog entry appears in the list of blogs.

Expected Outcome:
The new blog entry is created successfully and appears in the list of blogs.
